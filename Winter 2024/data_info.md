Dataset(s) used:
* [Most Successful Spotify Songs 2023](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/nelgiriyewithana/top-spotify-songs-2023?resource=download)
  * This dataset contains data on the features on the top 1000 most streamed songs of 2023.
* [Spotify 1.2M+ songs](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rodolfofigueroa/spotify-12m-songs/data)
  * This dataset contains data on the features of over 1.2 million songs from over 70 years of Spotify history.
